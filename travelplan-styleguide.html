Tokyo, Japan

3-day itinerary · 12–14 Jun 2026

Day 1 — Arrival & Asakusa
Morning Arrive at Narita Airport, check in to hotel in Shinjuku. Grab coffee at a local kissaten.
Afternoon Visit Senso-ji Temple and explore Nakamise-dori shopping street in Asakusa.
Evening Dinner at an izakaya in Shinjuku; walk through Omoide Yokocho (Memory Lane).
Day 2 — Shibuya & Harajuku
Morning Meiji Shrine, then stroll through Yoyogi Park.
Afternoon Harajuku's Takeshita Street for street fashion; Omotesando for cafés.
Evening Shibuya Crossing photo op; dinner in Shibuya or Ebisu area.
